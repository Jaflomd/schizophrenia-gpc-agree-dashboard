## Excluded or duplicate

- REC-0111 (already_excluded_in_verified_layer): The programme of research should use an adequately powered, multicentre, double-blind, randomised controlled design to test the physical health benefits, risks and costs of discontinuing or reducing antipsychotic medication among young adults with first episode psychosis who have achieved remission. The primary outcomes should be quality of life and metabolic disorder, including weight gain; secondary outcomes should include side effects, serious relapse, acceptability and user preference.
