# Recommendation consensus by AGREE-II quality band

This table compares recommendation categories across all 19 guidelines and across the preliminary AGREE-II quality bands requested by the review team. AGREE-II is used here as a guideline-methodology subgroup variable, not as the certainty of each individual recommendation.

Consensus cell format: `level: n/N guidelines (coverage); global signal; dominant action/action profile`.

## Band denominators

- All guidelines: 19
- `>60%`: 2 guidelines (APA 2020; VA/DoD 2024)
- `50-60%`: 6 guidelines (RANZCP 2016; Canada Dx 2017; Canada Psicosocial 2017; Canada Farmaco 2017; WHO mhGAP 2024; RANZCP GRADE 2026)
